## Prioritization Approach

Phased by **dependency and maintenance cost**, per PRD §10:

- **P0 — Foundation (v1.0):** the canonical record and the CMS that keeps it alive.
- **P1 — Differentiation (v1.5):** the intelligent layer, sequenced after the corpus exists.
- **P2 — Expansion (v2.0):** the platform features with recurring obligations, gated on proven sustainability.

Pulling a feature forward requires a PRD amendment and a [`../memory/DECISIONS.md`](../memory/DECISIONS.md) entry.
