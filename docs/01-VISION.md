# 01 — Vision

> Status: **Defined** — derived from the PRD ([`02-PRODUCT.md`](02-PRODUCT.md)), which is the authoritative product document.

## Purpose

Why Deepak Labs exists and what it aspires to become.

## The Vision

A single, permanent digital home where everything Deepak produces — research, engineering, publications, writing, and experience — lives as one coherent, continuously current system, owned outright rather than rented from platforms, and equally legible to a hiring manager skimming for 90 seconds, a researcher verifying a citation, and an AI answering on Deepak's behalf.

## The Problem

Deepak's professional record is fragmented across rented platforms (LinkedIn, GitHub, Scholar), each telling a partial, platform-shaped story. Generic portfolios cannot express the researcher–engineer dual identity, decay because updating them is painful, and are illegible to the AI systems that increasingly mediate discovery.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Canonical or nothing** — this is where the truth about Deepak's work lives; other platforms are mirrors.
2. **Signal in 90 seconds** — evaluators extract the essentials fast; depth is available, never required.
3. **Alive by design** — every surface updatable in minutes, or it doesn't ship.
4. **Machine-readable by default** — humans and AI consume the same structured record.
5. **One maintainer, forever** — recurring effort must justify itself or be cut.
6. **Restraint is the aesthetic** — fewer surfaces, deeper content.

## What Success Looks Like

- All external profiles link here, and this site is newer and deeper than any of them.
- A first-time evaluator understands who Deepak is and why it matters in under two minutes.
- The system is never more than a week behind Deepak's real life.
- The AI assistant answers about Deepak accurately, from his corpus only, and declines everything else.
- Any future human or AI can resume development from this repository's documentation alone.

## Non-Goals

Not a social network, not a multi-tenant platform, not a general news product, not monetized, not an experiments playground. See PRD §9.

## Time Horizon

- **Years 1–2:** the canonical record — complete, current, discoverable.
- **Years 3–5:** the knowledge graph — densely interlinked content with navigable trajectories.
- **Years 5+:** the agent-era presence — the authoritative structured source AI intermediaries consult about Deepak.
